## 📊 E-commerce sales and customer insight dashboard

## 🔍 Overview of project 

Through the analysis of a dataset, this project provides key insights regarding an e-commerce company’s sales performance, customer purchasing behaviour, return rates, and operational efficiency. The data-driven decision-making will be supported by a Power BI dashboard that provides interactivity. 

---

## 🎯 Business problem 

The e-commerce company is currently experiencing:

* Sales performance that is fluctuating 
* Certain product categories that are seeing higher return rates 
* Uncertainty of customer purchasing behaviours 

---

## 📌 Business objectives 

* Analysis of the sales trend and revenue drivers
* Understanding customer purchasing patterns
* Identifying reasons why products are being returned at high rates 
* Evaluating the effectiveness of discount strategies 
* Improving delivery performance and overall operational efficiency 

---

## 📂 Dataset description 

The dataset contains transactional data with the following fields:

* **OrderID** – Unique identifier for each order
* **CustomerID** – Unique identifier for each customer
* **OrderDate** – Date the order was placed 
* **Category** – Product category 
* **Price & Quantity** – Unit price and quantity purchased
* **TotalAmount** – Amount of money spent before a discount was applied
* **Discount and FinalAmount** – Amount of discount applied on an order and the final amount spent
* **PaymentMethod** – Method of payment used for the order
* **City** – Geographic location of the customer
* **DeliveryDays** – The length of time it took to deliver the product
* **Returned** – Indicates whether or not an order was returned (1 = returned, 0 = not returned) 

--
---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators

1) Revenues Totaling ₹651,000
2) Average Order Value (AOV) of ₹651
3) Return Rate at 51%
4) Highest Grossing Categories - Electronics & Home Goods


---

## 📈 List of Features Included in Dashboard

The Power BI Dashboard provides the following features: 

1) Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
2) Performance Analysis by Category
3) Revenue Distribution by City
4) Customer Analysis - Judging Between Repeat Vs. New Customers and AOV
5) Returns Analysis - Returns Rate Based on Category
6) Operational Metrics - Relationship Between Delivery Times and Returns
7) Discount Analysis - Discoutns/Promotion Affect on Sales

---

## 🔍 Business Insight Key Areas

**Sales Performance:** Monthly fluctuations indicate that sales trend seasonally; Electronics are leading categories for grossing revenues, while HomeGoods are lagging categories. 

**Customer Behavior:** Customer spending is moderate when comparing AOV (£651) with percentage of customers using online payment methods.

**Return Analysis:**  Return rates of 51% negatively affect profitability and there are high return rates in multiple categories.

**Operational Efficiency:** Higher delivery time is associated with more returns and delivery time/efficiency differs from city-to-city. 

**Discount Strategies:** While discounting does help to promote sales, overuse of discounts may contribute to reduced overall profitability.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Enhance Product Descriptions and Inspection Procedures to Decrease Returns.
2. Enhance Logistics to Decrease Delivery Times for Cities with High Delivery Slight Delay
3. Limited Amount of Discounts and Focus on Specific Discount Pricing Options
4. Create Retention Programs to Encourage Repeat Sales
5. Focus Attention on High Profitability High Grossing Category Areas.
